Urban Road Network Suitability & Expansion Planning (URSEP)

Evaluates future road corridors by analyzing connectivity gaps, congestion nodes, and expansion feasibility. Ensures scalable mobility infrastructure..

Dijkstra, Multi-Source BFS, KD-Tree, Range Tree, Radius Queries, Min-Heap

Critical Facility Coverage & Accessibility Planning (CFCAP)

Determines optimal placement for hospitals, police, and fire stations. Ensures all citizens fall within safe emergency response times.

Clustering (K-Means), Priority Queue, Fenwick Tree, Hash Map, Skip List, Greedy

Sanitation Infrastructure Layout & Public Cleanliness Planning (SIL-PCP)c

Designs sanitation zones, optimizes bin placements, and schedules waste collection efficiently based on density and usage patterns.

2D Prefix Sum, Segment Tree, Fenwick Tree, Sliding Window, Matrix DP

Multi-Structure Population Intelligence System (MSPIS)

Provides instant access to population density and demographic analytics across any region of the city grid. Supports real-time planning.

Quadtree, R-Tree, KD-Tree, Hash Table, Spatial Partitioning

Hierarchical Spatial Knowledge Grid (HSKG)

Stores all city assets (parks, schools, hospitals) in a layered spatial index. Enables fast “nearest facility” and urban gap detection.

Dynamic Programming, Greedy, Binary Search Trees, Heaps, Constraint Graphs

Intelligent Housing Distribution & Optimization Framework (IHDOF)

Allocates housing types (affordable, luxury, mixed-use) optimally under zoning, budget, and density constraints.

Backtracking, Constraint Satisfaction, Red-Black Tree, Rule Graphs, Pruning

Regulatory Compliance & Zoning Feasibility Solver (RC-ZFS)

Automatically validates zoning plans for compliance with building codes, buffer rules, and environmental constraints.

Priority Queue (Min-Heap)

Flood Risk Management & Hydrological Simulation System (FRMHSS)

Simulates rainfall runoff, flood spread patterns, and terrain-based water flow to identify high-risk urban zones. Helps planners place drainage systems, retention ponds, and emergency pathways effectively. Supports proactive flood mitigation for sustainable city resilience.

K-Means, Prim’s MST, DFS / BFS, Partition Logic, Edge Relaxation

>Multi-Facility Accessibility & Placement Optimizer (MFAPO)

Identifies optimal locations for public amenities like parks, PHCs, schools, and libraries based on city-wide accessibility metrics..

Segment Trees,union-find

Smart Urban Land-Use Conflict Detection & Resolution Engine (SULCD-RE)

Identifies conflicts between planned land uses—such as industrial zones too close to residential or schools placed near heavy-traffic corridors. Automatically suggests conflict-free zone adjustments to maintain safe, legal, and sustainable land development.
